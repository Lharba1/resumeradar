ResumeRadar helps immigrants navigate Canada's job market with AI tools: ATS resume optimizer, CV builder, job feed, cover letter generator, interview prep, and job tracker. Uniquely positioned at the intersection of **immigration + AI-powered career tools + bilingual (EN/FR)** — a niche with strong search intent and no dominant player.

**Differentiation:** No competitor combines ATS optimization + immigration context + bilingual EN/FR + Canadian market in one platform.

## E-E-A-T Strategy

### Current gaps (ordered by priority)

1. **No real author identity** — No founder/team name anywhere. "ResumeRadar Editorial Team" in schema is unverifiable. Fix: Add one real author with bio page, LinkedIn URL, Person schema.
2. **Unlinked statistics** — Stats Canada, IRCC, "95% of employers use ATS" stated without source links. Fix: Link every stat to primary source.
3. **Dead sameAs social links** — Organization schema references LinkedIn/Twitter/Product Hunt URLs that don't exist. Fix: Remove until accounts are created.
4. **Unverified testimonials** — First name only, no date, no platform. Fix: Add date + "verified user" label, or embed third-party reviews.
5. **No contact page** — Trust signal for Google. Add `/contact` with email form.

### E-E-A-T build plan

**Experience:** Feature real user stories with named outcomes. "Built by immigrants" section with real founding context.

**Expertise:** Author bio with credentials (HR background, Canadian job market experience). Cite Statistics Canada, IRCC guidelines, LinkedIn Workforce Report.

**Authoritativeness:** Get listed on newcomer resource pages (Settlement.org, ACCES Employment, MOSAIC). Guest posts on immigration blogs. Product Hunt launch.

**Trustworthiness:** Visible contact info, PIPEDA compliance, testimonials with full names and dates.

- [ ] Visible author byline + published date
