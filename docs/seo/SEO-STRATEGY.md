# ResumeRadar — SEO + GEO Strategy
**Business type:** SaaS (freemium)
**Target market:** Immigrants and international job seekers in Canada
**Language:** Bilingual (English + French)
**Date:** May 2026

---

## Business Context

ResumeRadar helps immigrants navigate Canada's job market with AI tools: ATS resume optimizer, CV builder, job feed, cover letter generator, interview prep, and job tracker. The product is uniquely positioned at the intersection of **immigration + AI-powered career tools** — a niche with strong search intent and no dominant player.

## Competitor Landscape

| Competitor | Strength | Weakness |
|------------|----------|----------|
| Jobscan | Strong brand, ATS leader | Not immigration-focused, expensive |
| Resume.io | Clean UX, global reach | No ATS optimization, no Canada focus |
| Rezi.ai | AI-first positioning | No immigrant angle, US-focused |
| Enhancv | Good templates | No ATS tool, no bilingual |
| Job Bank Canada | Government authority | No AI, no CV tools |

**ResumeRadar's gap:** No product combines ATS optimization + immigration context + bilingual (EN/FR) + Canadian market in one platform.

---

## E-E-A-T Strategy

**Experience:** Feature real user stories — "Yassine went from 0 callbacks to 3 interviews in 2 weeks." Anonymized success metrics.

**Expertise:** Blog posts authored by named HR professionals, recruiters, or immigration consultants. Cite Statistics Canada data, IRCC guidelines.

**Authoritativeness:** Get listed on newcomer resource pages (Settlement.org, ACCES Employment, MOSAIC). Guest posts on immigration blogs.

**Trustworthiness:** Privacy policy, PIPEDA compliance badge, data retention policy visible on upload page. Testimonials with full names.
